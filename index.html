What is OpEx? Operating expenses (OpEx) are everything it costs to run the property. This model uses 55% of gross revenue as a blended OpEx rate — inclusive of franchise royalties (~7%), brand and marketing fees (~9%), payroll, utilities, laundry, insurance, repairs & maintenance, merchant processing, and miscellaneous costs. The remaining 45% is your Net Operating Income (NOI) — the money available to cover your loan payment.
